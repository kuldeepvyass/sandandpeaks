Why You Need To Go On This Tour?

The mysterious valleys and clear river water is a must visit. The sight of the high snow capped mountains of Sikkim will give you a sense of awe. Bestowed with natural beauty and breath taking sights Sikkim also has a traditional and cultural touch.

Day 1
Bagdogra airport to Gangtok (125 Kms | 4 Hours)
• Pick-up from Bagdogra Airport.
• It takes about 4 hours to reach Gangtok from Bagdogra airport.
• Upon arrival, we'll check into our hotel accommodation, unpack, and get freshened up.
• Post our break, we'll start again for Gangtok local city Tour
• The rest of the evening will be spent browsing souvenirs, local items, and more in the shopping district of Gangtok market.
• Take a stroll in the famous M G Marg during your evening free time. This place is magical and has plenty of good restaurants and cafes!
• We'll return to our hotel around evening time. • The day ends here, and we'll spend the night in Gangtok.

Day 2
Discover Gangtok: Tsongmo Lake|Baba Mandir|Nathu La ( Closed on Monday & Tuesday)
• You'll wake up with the smell of delicious breakfast, and we'll gather around for the first meal of the day.
• Today we will go for Excursion to Tsomgo Lake & Baba Mandir.
• It is nearly 40 kilometers - The Lake is oval shaped lake nearly 50 feet deep - Generally covered in snow for most of the year the year.
• Nearby is the sacred Baba Mandir known to be a very holy site for all.
• Nathu La Pass to Republic of China is nearly 20 kilometers and is optional tour ( 4000 rupees will be applicable for this pass).
• Evening free to roam around M G Road (Mall) or local Shopping center. Over Night stay at Gangtok.
Day 3
Gangtok Full Day Sightseeing
• After breakfast drive for the city tour of Gangtok.
• Sight seeing will be for half day hence the drive will be easy and comfortable.
• Visit to Directorate of Handicrafts & Handloom, Research Institute of Tibetology, Do Drul Chorten (Stupa), Enchey Monastery, White Hall, Flower Show, Ganesh Tok, Tashi View Point, Banjhakri Falls, one of the finest falls close to Gangtok, etc.
• Evening free to roam around M G Road (Mall).
• There are few discotheques in Gangtok to spend time,
• Over Night stay at Gangtok.
Day 4
Gangtok to Darjeeling Transfer
• After breakfast at hotel and then forwarding journey to Darjeeling.
• Darjeeling takes nearly 135 Kilometers 3 – 3 1⁄2 hours.
• On arrival check-in to your hotel and have rest.
• It is situated at an height of 2123 meters / 70001 feet on the top of a ridge with spectacular views of the Mount Everest is popularly known as the Queen of the hills and known for Orthodox tea.
• Check into hotel – Evening free for leisure time.
• Over Night stay at Darjeeling.
Day 5
Darjeeling Local Sight Seeing (Full Day)
• Early morning at around 5 AM drive to Tiger Hill to view sunrise over Kanchendzonga Peak (subject to clear weather)
• Don't forget to visit Ghoom Monastery, Peace memorial & Batasia Loop on the route.
• After visiting we will come back to hotel for breakfast.
• After breakfast we will go to see remaining places of Darjeeling.
• Half Day city tour includes Padmaja Naidu Zoological Park, Himalayan Mountaineering Institute (Closed on Thursday), Ropeway, Tensing Gumpo Rock, Tibetan Refugee Self Help Center (Closed on Sunday), Tea Estate, Natural History Museum (Sunday Closed).
• Back to hotel – Evening free to stroll around the area
• Overnight stay at Darjeeling
Day 6
Time To Say Goodbye...Departure From Bagdogra
• Enjoy a tasty breakfast while discussing 'How Great The Last 05 Days Went' with your partner.
• Exchange numbers, make promises to meet sometime in the future and bid your farewell to everyone you have known for the last four days.
• After that, we'll drop you at Bagdogra Airport, which will be your departing point.
• Tour ends here.

FAQ's

Is Sikkim Good for Honeymoon?
Yes! Sikkim is one of the best honeymoon destinations for couples planning their honeymoon. It falls in a region that has the best of both worlds, mountains and beaches. So, there’s no need to choose. It is widely known for its special Spa treatment, which you can try for relaxation. Moreover, it is one out of two destinations in India where you’ll find a houseboat.
What to Wear on Honeymoon in Sikkim?
There’s no restriction when it comes to clothing. Choose something light and breezy. How you want to dress depends totally on your preferences. There’s one clause, though: you cannot wear bikinis on open beaches such as Kovalam beach and others.
How to Enjoy Honeymoon in Sikkim?
The best way to enjoy a honeymoon trip to Sikkim is by planning ahead of time, leaving you hassle-free while you’re on your honeymoon. The second thing is to go for a houseboat stay, visiting Alleppey, Cochin, Thekkady, and tea plantations in Munnar.
How to Plan a Honeymoon Trip in Sikkim?
The best way is to contact Sand and Peaks for that. We’ll make sure that accommodation, transportation, and all other activities are booked on time and without hassle.
Is Sikkim Safe for Honeymoon?
The literacy rate of Sikkim is higher than other Indian states, and matriarchy is followed on a general note, which makes it one of the safest places to travel in India. You’ll find generous and great people in the state of Sikkim.
