About Us

Sand and Peaks was built to personalize the tourism space.

So, we set out to create a firm that sells more than just packages, and we wanted to distribute 'Value.' something that feels like we're a part of your trip rather than just a facilitator for the same.

We saw that everyone in this space was very interested in selling packages just for the sake of mere profit and found ourselves not in sync with that fact.

What do we do differently, you ask?


Well! On the upfront, we're just the same as your everyday tour operator, but inside we're made up by the people who are not fueled by money. But are driven by the love for finding new things, making things more interesting, bringing change, and making sure that everyone ends up with a smile in the end.


Most of our deals are facilitated by including the local individuals of a destination to boost local tourism. While at the same time, we're totally against the idea of discounts and that's why we offer none.


Once again, you ask why? See! The chain of discounts is something that would end up with someone being unhappy with the price they are paid for their services down the lane, and thus, there's a chance of them faltering on what's promised, agreed?


Now, that's something we don't want, remember we're all about making everyone happy. So, be it the people we do business with or the customer, we bank on building relationships more than we bank on building a company.


That's the secret recipe which helps us cook the best of trips for you.


We want to innovate, create and renovate the word 'tourism' meant for anyone. This is why we are starting our footprint in India and aiming at global recognition in the coming few years.


Let's see where the next adventure takes us.


-Team Sand and Peaks
