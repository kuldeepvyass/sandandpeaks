Why You Need To Go On This Tour?

The beautiful Dal lake & Mughal Gardens are must visit. The sight of the The snow-capped mountains fill the region with exquisite surroundings and the snow-capped trees look awesome while offering a serene feeling to everyone Bestowed with natural beauty and breath taking sights Kashmir also has a traditional and cultural touch.

Day 1
Arrival & Gulmarg Stay
• Pick-up from Sheikh Ul Alam International Airport in Srinagar.
• We will be driving for 2 hours to reach Gulmarg while crossing Srinagar.
• We arrive at a small picture-sue market town of Tangmarg and drive ahead on a scenic drive of 14 kilometers to Gulmarg
• The drive past colorful villages and rice fields, gives you an insight of the rich cultural past of Kashmir.
• We arrive in Gulmarg in the afternoon and check in at the hotel.
• Enjoy your day by gondola ride ( on your own Expenses)
• Later visit Drung Waterfall & back to your stay by the evening.
• Dinner and night halt at Gulmarg

Day 2
Gulmarg to Srinagar Transfer ( 54 kms ). Srinagar sightseeing.
• As usual, we'll start our day with a delicious breakfast.
• After breakfast, we will leave for Srinagar.
• On arrival, transfer to hotel Afternoon, leave for local sightseeing of Srinagar City.
• We will Visit Mughal gardens: - Chesma Shahi (Mughal Spring), Nishat (The Garden of Bliss), Shalimar (The Abode of Love).
• We'll go for dinner in the evening.
• The day ends here, and we'll spend the night in Srinagar Hotel.
Day 3
Srinagar to Pahalgam, Visit Awantipura Ruins, Saffron Feilds (148 kms on way)
• You'll wake up with the smell of delicious breakfast, and we'll gather around for the first meal of the day.
• Post breakfast, we'll check out from the hotel and set out for Pahalgam.
• Stop at saffron fields of Pampore, Avantipura Ruins and the village of Bijbehara and drive through the second largest city of Anantnag.
• Lastly, we'll head towards our accommodation in Pahalgam.
• Post-check-in, we'll freshen up and get ready for dinner.
• The day ends here, and we all go back to bed in our hotel rooms in Pahalgam.
Day 4
Pahalgam to Srinagar transfers & PM Shikara ride, Srinagar Rider
• We'll have an early breakfast, and post that we'll leave for Srinagar.
• Upon arrival, we'll check into our hotel/Houseboat accommodation, unpack, and get freshened up.
• Post our break, we'll start again for Enjoy Floating Gardens, Vegetable Gardens, Golden Lake, Inner markets like Mena Bazar, and Canals etc, also enjoy the scenic view of Zabarwan Mountains,
• Enjoy shikara ride in Dal lake around evening time, and we'll go for dinner.
• The day ends here, and we'll spend the night in Srinagar Houseboat.
Day 5
Srinagar to Sonamarg Sightseeing & Back to Srinagar (89 kms on way)
• Today, we'll enjoy breakfast while sitting with a valley view in our hotel.
• Later in the day, we'll move out to enjoy leave for Sonamarg for a Day trip
• The drive from Srinagar to Sonamarg is very beautiful as you will pass through some towns and villages.
• In Sonamarg you can visit Thajwas Glacier which is around 4Kms from Sonamarg, a Local Taxi Or Pony Ride has to be hired (on your own Expenses)
• In the evening, we'll back to Srinagar & explore the local market to collect souvenirs.
• Post dinner time, we'll have an overnight stay at a hotel in Srinagar.
Day 6
Time to Say Goodbye... Departure From Srinagar
• Enjoy a tasty breakfast while discussing 'How Great The Last 05 Days Went' with your partner.
• After that, we'll drop you at Sheikh Ul Alam International Airport, which will be your departing point.
• Tour ends here.
