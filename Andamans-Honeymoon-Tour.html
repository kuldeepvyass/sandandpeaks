Why You Need To Go On This Tour?


This package is made for your special one for you to take them away on a getaway which is surreal and blissful. Andaman and Nicobar is a place which is quite serene and offbeat when compared to the touristic culture in rest of India. The place is quite offbeat but is blessed with natural beauty that will surely take your heart away.

Port Blair is the capital city of Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Cellular Jail or Kaala Paani is one of the most popular places to visit in Port Blair. A trip around the jail blocks of the Cellular Jail will take you on an experience where the freedom fighters were imprisoned during the freedom fight against the Britishers.

Neil Island is an island of the Andaman and Nicobar which is located in Ritchie’s Archipelago. Neil Islands have one of the most beautiful and scenic beaches in the Andamans, most famous being Laxmanpur Beach and Bharatpur Beach.

Arrival and Sightseeing Tour Of Port Blair
• Arrival at Veer Savarkar Airport.
• Airport pick-up & move to hotel and little relax.
• In the evening we will visit Jail Visit Memorial museum, Carbyn's Cove Beach.
• In the evening move towards to view the history of the heroic freedom struggle which is brought alive in a moving Light & Sound    show at the Cellular Jail.
• After that, drop to Hotel.
• Overnight stay at Port Blair.

Swaraj Dweep & Radha Nagar Beach Sightseeing
• After Breakfast, Early morning departure by cruise to Swaraj Dweep (Havelock Island).
• It is 38km by sea from Port Blair.
• Arrival at Havelock Island, our representative will take you to your resort.
• In the afternoon, we will go for sightseeing to beach no. 7 (Radha Nagar Beach).
• In the evening, return back to Hotel.
• Night stay at Havelock Island.

Day 3
Neil Island ( Laxmanpur Beach, Bharatpur Beach & Natural Bridge)
• Breakfast will be served in the morning.
• After that we will move for Neil island by Cruise .
• Our representative will receive you at Neil Island and will drop you to the Resort.
• Have some leisure time and proceed for Neil Island sightseeing.
• We will visit Laxmanpur Beach, Bharatpur Beach & Howrah Bridge.
• After complete back to resort.
• Overnight stay at Neil island.
Day 4
Neil Island (Sahiddweep Island) to Port Blair
• Have breakfast in Neil Island and return back to Port Blair by Green Ocean Cruise / Makruzz.
• On arriavl check-in to your hotel.
• Enjoy leisure day in Port Blair.
• In the evening explore local market & back to your hotel.
• Overnight stay at Port Blair.
Day 5
Departure From Port Blair
• Breakfast will be served in the morning.
• The guests pack their bags and get ready for a checkout.
• Post check-out from the hotel, guests will be dropped at Port Blair International Airport.
• Guests will board the pre-booked flights at the designated timings.
• The tour will end here.
• Note: If your departure flight is a delay, the additional tour will be arranged on a direct payment basis.
